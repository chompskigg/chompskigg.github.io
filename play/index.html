Outside of research, here's some other stuff I've made

ElseWhere

In ElseWhere you play a young girl named Els as she finds herself in the eternal night world of Bohr. Explore the surrounding forests and buildings to uncover the secrets of Fireless and the eternal winter.

I worked on this game in grad school with a small team over the course of 16 weeks. It's only meant to be a demo, but you can fully play through to the end. Check out our itch.io page!

iSpace Explorer

In my New Media Capstone, I served as the solo developer and team lead for an Apple Vision Pro app designed to enhance and engage young audiences in space education. The app, iSpace, enables users to explore information, interact with physics objects, and test their knowledge through quizzes.

I developed iSpace using Apple's Xcode and SwiftUI within the VisionOS environment. Since the Vision Pro had not yet been released when I began development, it was a fun challenge to work through. iSpace is currently on the app store and you can check out the GitHub repo too!

Guided bullet mod

I spent some time learning LUA scripting to create an addon for Garry's Mod on Steam. Players can shoot a revolver and control the bullet from a third-person perspective, as if they were the bullet itself. The steam addon has over 16,000 downloads!

Fantasy Minecraft Datapack

Over the course of two years, I collaborated with a small team of friends to develop a large-scale Minecraft datapack. From Orcs and Elves to Wizards and Alchemists, players have a wide variety of play options alongside hundreds of custom items and new abilities to uncover throughout their adventures.

The datapack is still a work in progress, and we continue to update it with each new Minecraft release. If you'd like to check out our progress, please check out our GitHub repo and datapack showcase video.
